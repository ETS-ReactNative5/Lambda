  1. Tell me about a time when you were not able to meet a time commitment. What prevented you from meeting it? What was the outcome and what did you learn from it?
  2. Describe a long-term project that you managed. How did you keep everything moving along in a timely manner?
  3. Give me an example of a time when you set a goal and were able to meet or achieve it

Adaptation

  1. Tell me about a time you had to quickly adjust your work priorities to meet changing demands.

Team / decision

  1. an example when you had to push back to HQ or challenged a decision
  2. Tell me about the toughest decision you’ve had to make in the past six months
  3. Tell me about a decision that you regret.

Team / leadership

  1. What did you do when you needed to motivate a group of individuals?
  2. Tell me about a time you stepped up into a leadership role

Team / communication & negotiation

  1. Do you collaborate well?
  2. Describe a situation when you negotiated with others in your organization to reach agreement.

Team / coworkers

  1. We’ve all had to work with people that don’t like us. How do you deal with someone that doesn’t like you?
  2. We all make mistakes we wish we could take back. Tell me about a time you wish you’d handled a situation differently with a colleague.
  3. The last time you had to apologize to someone

Team / conflict

  1. Give me an example of a time you faced a conflict while working on a team. How did you handle that?
  2. Tell me about a time when you received negative feedback from your manager. How did you respond?

Problem solving

  1. Tell me about a time when you missed an obvious solution to a problem
  2. A time when you faced a problem that had multiple possible solutions
  3. Tell me about a time when you came up with a new approach to a problem.
  4. Describe a time when you anticipated potential problems and developed preventive measures.
  5. Describe a situation in which you found a creative way to overcome an obstacle.

Strategy / data

  1. How have you leveraged data to develop a strategy?
  2. a time when you were 75% through a project, & you had to pivot strategy
  3. Tell me about a time when you had to choose between technologies for a project
  4. Tell me about a time you had to deal with ambiguity

Innovation

  1. What’s the most innovative new idea that you have implemented?

Ownership principle

  1. Describe a time when you sacrificed short term goals for long term success
  2. Provide an example of when you personally demonstrate ownership.

Strength / weakness

  1. What’s your greatest strength
  2. Biggest weakness

Clients

  1. We all deal with difficult customers from time to time. Tell me about a challenging client-facing situation and how you handled it.
  2. How do you show customer obsession?
